About Us

ArtsTech's mission is to better the lives of under-served urban youth through the development of marketable artistic, technical, and educational skills. ArtsTech accomplishes this by operating a center for youth enterprise that provides educational services and hands-on training in fine arts and digital literacy, and by building and managing collaborative partnerships that use art and technology as educational tools for the benefit of youth in the community.
